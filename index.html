I'm a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at HTC DeepQ. My work involves Computer Vision (CV) and Natural Language Processing (NLP) using Deep Learning. Prior to joining HTC, I was a machine learning researcher at the Computational Imaging Lab in the Sheba Medical Center, Israel (top-9 hospital worldwide).

I obtained my M.Sc. from Tel Aviv University under the supervision of Prof. Nahum Kiryati and Dr. Arnaldo Mayer. My research focus was in medical imaging with the addition of medical report processing.

In addition to my publications, I am experienced with developing large language models (LLM's), and I'm familiar with outlier detection techniques for CV and NLP applications. I have also worked with knowledge-distillation for semi-supervised learning as well as flow-based image generation models. Feel free to contact me via email for inquiries.
